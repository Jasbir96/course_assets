200px

This div will always occupy 200px x 200px no matter how you change shape and size of browser

20vh and 20vw

The v in vw and vh stands for viewport, which is user's visible area of a web page. h in vh stands for height and w in vw stands for width.So when we say 20vw and 20vh, the div will occupy 20% of the user's visible area of web page. That means if you change shape and size of browser the size of div will also change.

50% of my parent

We can give sizes in terms of percentage, in that case the current element to which we are applying css rule will look to its parent and get x% of its parents size. This is also called inheritance and can also be seen in fonts.
