Title: 
Dynamic Game

Meta-Tags:
Javascript, JS, interview, questions, interview questions,variables,scope,practice

Description:
Find output of the following:


const num = 5;
console.log(num + 5);
let a = 6;
a = a + num;
console.log(num - a);

Options:
A)  10
    -6

B)  Error

C)  10
    -16

D)  10
    -10

Solution:
A
